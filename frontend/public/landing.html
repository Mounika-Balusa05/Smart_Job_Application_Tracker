🔄 How It Works

Your Job Search Made Simple

From applying to getting hired — here's how JobTrackr helps you at every step of your journey.

01
📝
Register & Login

Create your free account securely with email and password. Protected with JWT tokens and bcrypt hashing — fully private and secure.

02
📋
Add Applications

Add every job you apply to with company name, role, status, priority, salary, location, recruiter contact and personal notes.

03
🔄
Track Status

Update status as you progress — Applied → Screening → Interview → Offer or Rejected. Filter and search your applications anytime.

04
📅
Schedule Interviews

Link interviews to applications. Add type, date, time, duration, meeting link and interviewer details — everything in one place.

05
🔔
Get Reminders

In-app notifications alert you 1 day and 1 hour before every interview automatically — so you never miss an important round.

06
📊
View Dashboard

See your full job search at a glance — total applications, interviews scheduled, offers received and upcoming interviews for the week.
